flex display

mobile app

About the app

Use “flex display” to turn your phone into a strong display controller. wirelessly extend, mirror and share your phone screen with other phone (or devices). connect up to 4 devices at the same time to built your picture puzzle or any kind of media. You can use other phone to extent your screen or easily play a jigsaw puzzle game with your firends.

App idea

The idea mainly came from Nova LED screen controller. With this software professionals map several led panel to generate greater image. Also sharing display and Air display have inspired me to put all this cool features into one app.
In fact all these app mirror your display and show exactly what you can see in master device. They designed to be used as second screen, but with this app you can also have strong display controller and display partial of a picture on each display.
This app uses one phone as a master to control other devices. Now client devices need to stay in receive mode to got the data.
