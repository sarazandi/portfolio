About me

As a data-driven, passionate product designer with a background in interaction design and software development, I thrive on my ability to effectively communicate technical design requirements. The result successfully bridges the gap between design and development to achieve a more robust final product.

My process is hands-on, collaborative and iterative. I leverage user research, create user personas, design user flows and wireframes, prototypes, and conduct user testing. I currently code in HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Early on in my career, I learned about the power of storytelling through a camera lens. This led me to complete my Master's in photography and film. The process of film making was a great segway into building software products as it was a crash course in collaborative teamwork.

Recently I've focused on freelance projects while raising my two beautiful children. Being a mother has taught me that parents are naturally user experience designers. Communication and feedback are the keys in any community, especially when trying to foster a creative, smart, and humble family unit.
